Below is a concise, research-based profile of Applied Digital, compiled from a crawl of applieddigital.com and linked investor materials.

Organization summary
- Identity and mission: Applied Digital (Nasdaq: APLD) designs, builds, and operates purpose-built, high‑performance data centers and cloud services for AI, HPC, cloud, networking, and blockchain workloads. The company positions itself as “Different by Design” with infrastructure engineered specifically for accelerated compute, energy efficiency, and rapid deployment.
- Core business focus:
  - AI/HPC data centers: Purpose-built “AI Factories” with high power density, advanced liquid cooling, InfiniBand networking, and sites selected near renewable/stranded power to optimize PUE and sustainability.
  - Applied Digital Cloud: Turnkey GPU-as-a-Service (CaaS) and SuperComputer-as-a-Service with bare metal, Slurm, Kubernetes, and containerized environments. Offers NVIDIA H100/H200, A100, A40, RTX A6000; claims up to 3.2 Tbps InfiniBand per node; “NVIDIA Preferred Cloud Partner” status.
- Target customers/industries: Hyperscalers and large AI companies training or serving models at scale; enterprises needing cost‑effective, high‑performance GPU compute; organizations running AI/ML, rendering, and broader HPC workloads.
- Value propositions:
  - Purpose-built for AI/HPC (vs. retrofitted general-purpose data centers).
  - Advanced liquid cooling and proprietary waterless cooling approaches to support high-density deployments with low PUE and near-zero water consumption (per investor press release for Polaris Forge 2).
  - Strategic siting near renewable/stranded power for cost and sustainability.
  - Speed to market and scalability (large development pipeline; contiguous design for low latency).
  - Cost efficiency versus traditional hyperscalers (as stated on Applied Digital Cloud page).
- Partnerships and ecosystem:
  - Hardware/OEM and infrastructure partners highlighted include NVIDIA, Dell Technologies, HPE, Supermicro, Cray; storage/software partners (e.g., WEKA, Croit); membership in the AI Alliance.
  - Testimonials include NVIDIA and ZEDEDA; Together AI cites collaboration to provide scalable capacity to its customers.

- Headquarters and footprint:
  - Corporate HQ: Dallas, Texas (3811 Turtle Creek Blvd., Suite 2100, Dallas, TX 75219).
  - Major campus developments in North Dakota (Polaris Forge 1 & 2; Ellendale and Harwood areas).
- Recent announcements and scale metrics:
  - $5B lease agreement (Oct 22, 2025) with a U.S.-based investment-grade hyperscaler for 200 MW at Polaris Forge 2 (approx. 15-year term), with right of first refusal for an additional 800 MW at the 1 GW campus; total leased capacity in ND with two global hyperscalers reaches 600 MW. Polaris Forge 2 spans 900+ acres, projected PUE 1.18, near-zero water consumption, phased online starting 2026 (Investor press release).
  - Additional 150 MW lease with CoreWeave at Polaris Forge 1 noted in the same release; homepage references a 250 MW agreement at Ellendale and executive media coverage.
  - Build/pipeline metrics highlighted on homepage: 480+ MW constructed in 18 months; 400 MW in active construction; 1.4+ GW future site pipeline.
  - Recognition: “Best Data Center in the Americas 2025” by Datacloud (per investor press release).

Distinctive aspects
- Purpose-built AI “Factory” campuses sited near renewable/stranded power with advanced liquid cooling and low PUE, designed for high-density GPU deployments and fast scale.
- Notable scale and speed: Rapid build-out (hundreds of MW active/constructed) with multi‑billion‑dollar, multi‑hundred‑MW leases to major hyperscalers; 1+ GW campus designs and multi‑GW pipeline.
- Cloud offerings with “NVIDIA Preferred Cloud Partner” status, multi‑stack deployment options, and high-performance networking (up to 3.2 Tbps InfiniBand per node).
