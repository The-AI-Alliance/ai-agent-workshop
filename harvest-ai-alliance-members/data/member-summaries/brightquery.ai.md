Below is a concise profile of BrightQuery (BQ), compiled from a crawl of brightquery.ai and key internal pages.

Summary
- Identity and mission: BrightQuery (BQ) bills itself as “The Factual AI Company,” with a mission to “make the world more factual, one dataset at a time.” It provides verified, up-to-date financial, employment, payroll, and firmographic data on U.S. companies and builds AI platforms that leverage this data for reliable, explainable insights.
- Core focus: Factual AI—combining verified public-sector filings and other sources with AI to deliver accurate, bias-mitigated insights. BQ positions data quality and validation at the center of its AI stack.
- Products and platforms:
  - Agentic Hybrid RAG Platform: Real-time data fusion for a complete, bias-free view.
  - Predictive Hybrid RAG Platform: Forecasts trends, detects anomalies, and manages risk.
  - Neuro-Symbolic Multi-Agent Platform: Collaborative, explainable AI for complex analysis.
  - Factual Chat: A conversational RAG interface grounded in validated BQ data frameworks, aimed at precise, context-aware responses.
  - Data products and filters: Monthly-updated, verified identity (EIN/DOL), industry classification (IRS/DOL/NAICS/SIC), precise employment and IRS-based financials; rich filters for growth, size/age, geography, credit risk, hierarchy, legal structure, and sector/industry analysis.
- Data foundation and methodology:
  - Verification sources: U.S. federal and state/local filings, including IRS and Department of Labor, plus >80,000 state and local agencies (Accuracy).
  - Breadth: 360° business view—corporate family trees, ownership, legal status, KYC/KYB, detailed financials, executives/contacts, branches/locations (Breadth).
  - Coverage (as stated on site): All U.S. companies and their owners tracked; claims include 70M companies, 106M legal entities, 300M locations, 230M contacts; another section lists 70M private firms, 5K public firms, 97M legal entities, 180M establishments, 154M employees, 150M officers, and 80K jurisdictions.
  - Proprietary imputation: A methodology derived from 10+ years of collaborative academic research with U.S. statistical agencies; validated via extensive back-testing with the IRS (per About page).
- Target customers and industries:
  - Financial services and capital markets, private equity, hedge funds, investment banks, commercial banks, insurance/insurtech, fintech.
  - Compliance and KYC/KYB/due diligence; underwriting and risk management.
  - Sales and marketing (go-to-market targeting); corporate and industry/regional analysis.
  - Government policy and defense/national security (situational awareness, forecasting).
- Adoption and scale (claimed on site): BQ data is used by leading hedge funds, PE firms, investment banks, commercial banks, insurers, fintechs, and large enterprises; in use by 300,000+ investment professionals, 200,000+ bankers, and 50,000+ compliance officers.
- Leadership and background:
  - Founder & CEO: Jose M. Plehn (Plehn-Dujowich), Ph.D. (Economics, University of Chicago); prior faculty roles at UCLA Anderson, UC Berkeley Haas, Temple University, SUNY Buffalo; extensive research collaborations with U.S. statistical agencies; past IP contributions to Powerlytics. Serves on the Board of Directors of the AI Alliance; oversees BQ’s open data initiatives.
  - Company timeline: BQ founded in 2019. BQ AI subsidiary (focused on AI initiatives) launched in 2023. Academic research roots date to 2006.
- Regions served: U.S.-centric coverage (“all U.S. companies & their owners”) with stated plans to expand datasets into global markets.

Distinctive aspects
- Verified-by-filings data at national scale (IRS/DOL/80K+ agencies) baked into an AI stack designed to mitigate bias and deliver explainable results.
- Proprietary imputation methodology validated against IRS benchmarks; emphasis on academic rigor and reproducibility.
- Hybrid RAG and neuro-symbolic multi-agent platforms plus a factual, RAG-based chat interface rooted in verified data.
- Strong sector focus for high-stakes use cases (finance, government/policy, defense/national security) and broad commercial applications.
