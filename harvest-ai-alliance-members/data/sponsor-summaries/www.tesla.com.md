Tesla, Inc. is a U.S.-based company headquartered in Austin, Texas that designs and manufactures electric vehicles and clean energy products to accelerate the world’s transition to sustainable energy. Its core offerings span EVs (Model S, 3, X, Y, and Cybertruck), energy generation and storage (Solar Roof/solar panels, Powerwall for homes, and Megapack for utilities), and software such as Autopilot and Full Self-Driving, supported by the Supercharger network and the NACS charging standard. Tesla serves consumers, businesses, and utilities globally and operates large-scale Gigafactories across the U.S., Europe, and China. Distinctives include deep vertical integration across hardware, software, and manufacturing, a vast fast‑charging network, and ongoing AI/autonomy R&D (e.g., Dojo supercomputer and Optimus robotics).

- Mission: “to accelerate the world’s transition to sustainable energy.”
- Core offerings: EVs (S/3/X/Y, Cybertruck); energy (Solar Roof/panels, Powerwall, Megapack); software (Autopilot/FSD); charging (Supercharger, NACS).
- HQ and footprint: Austin, Texas; Gigafactories in the U.S., Europe, and China; global customer base.
- Distinctives: vertical integration; large Supercharger network and open NACS standard; active AI/autonomy programs.
