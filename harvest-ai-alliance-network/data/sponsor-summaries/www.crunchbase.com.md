## Crunchbase — Company Profile

Crunchbase provides private company intelligence for go-to-market teams, investors, analysts, and founders. Its platform combines proprietary data with AI and predictive models to help users discover, qualify, and act on high‑potential companies faster than traditional, historical-data–only tools.

Core focus and offerings
- Crunchbase Pro (discovery and execution)
  - Search 4M+ private companies with AI‑powered natural language search.
  - View “live” financial data, heat scores, funding data, and financials to strengthen market understanding and identify high‑growth companies.
  - Reduce research time with instant summaries from an AI agent on company profiles.
  - Track milestones in a Kanban‑style board, add notes and tags, and export up to 2,000 rows/month for analysis.
  - Automatic alerts on company changes to be first to act on market shifts.
- Crunchbase Business (predictive intelligence)
  - Predictive insights for private companies, including funding predictions and risk forecasts.
  - Actionable signals designed to inform outreach, investment, and partnership decisions before public announcements.

Who it serves
- GTM teams: Pinpoint companies with buying power and tailor messaging to boost marketing and sales performance.
- Investors: Uncover high‑potential investment opportunities and monitor market advancements.
- Analysts: Track competitors and trends with trusted, real‑time insights.
- Founders: Access proprietary private‑market information to guide fundraising and strategic decisions.

Positioning and value propositions
- From static to predictive: Emphasizes a shift beyond static, historical data to “live, predictive, dynamic” insights.
- Proprietary data: Crunchbase’s data underpins product features, AI summaries, and predictive signals (e.g., Funding Predictions).
- Actionable and fast: Combines discovery, qualification, and engagement in one workflow—with AI and automation to accelerate execution.
