## Slack — Company Profile

Slack is a collaboration platform that brings people, information, apps, and AI agents together to help teams work more efficiently. It enables real-time and asynchronous communication (channels, threads, voice/video), integrates thousands of business apps, and now layers in AI features and agents to automate tasks, summarize information, and surface knowledge across an organization and its external partners.

Core focus and offerings
- Collaboration hub
  - Channels, messaging, file sharing, and voice/video to organize work by topic, project, or team.
  - Slack Connect to collaborate securely with external organizations (customers, partners).
- AI features and agents
  - AI summarization for threads and channels, meeting/action item extraction, and AI search answers.
  - Side-by-side AI agents that can help automate workflows and support domain-specific tasks.
- Platform and integrations
  - 2,500+ apps and a robust API to bring workflows into Slack (e.g., customer feedback, IT tickets, approvals).
  - Marketplace with thousands of apps and a global partner ecosystem.
- Automation and workflows
  - Built-in low-code/no-code tools to automate multi-step processes and reduce manual work.

Customers, scale, and impact (as reported on Slack’s site)
- 200,000+ paid customers; daily active users in 150+ countries.
- 77 of the Fortune 100 use Slack; 80% of the Fortune 100 use Slack Connect.
- Outcomes and usage highlights include:
  - 47% increase in productivity for teams using Slack (customer tracking survey).
  - 35% average time saved due to automation for Slack users.
  - AI features can save users ~97 minutes per week on average.
  - 700M messages sent daily; 4M Slack Connect users work with external teams weekly; 3M daily workflows; 1.7M apps actively used weekly.
- Recognitions: Slack is cited as a leader across 340+ G2 market reports (e.g., business messaging, workflow/project management, unified workspaces).

Ecosystem and programs
- Partners: Collaborations with leading SaaS providers and 1,000+ products shaping the future of work.
- Slack Marketplace: Centralized catalog of Slack apps and integrations.
- Slack Fund: A venture fund (via Salesforce Ventures) investing in startups building the next generation of software.
- Careers and values:
  - Mission-driven culture focused on making people’s working lives simpler, more pleasant, and more productive.
  - Slack for Good initiative to increase representation of historically underrepresented individuals in technology.

Presence and offices
- Global operations; teams work in Slack and from Salesforce offices around the world. (Office locations are listed via Salesforce.com.)

Recent positioning themes
- “Bring your people, apps, and AI agents together”: unified collaboration with AI-native features to find knowledge, automate tasks, and drive outcomes.
- External collaboration at enterprise scale through Slack Connect.
