Here is a concise profile of NOVAworks based on a crawl of its public site.

Summary
NOVAworks is the public workforce development leader for Silicon Valley and San Mateo County, serving all of San Mateo County and the Santa Clara County cities of Cupertino, Los Altos, Milpitas, Mountain View, Palo Alto, Santa Clara, and Sunnyvale. As the region’s workforce board, it coordinates employers, education, community partners, and job seekers to address local labor market needs, drive innovation, and expand equitable access to high-quality careers.

NOVAworks delivers no‑cost services for businesses, career seekers, and young adults:
- Business services: talent acquisition via the NOVAworks Job Connector (with real‑time skill‑based candidate matching), hiring and employer events, skills‑based hiring support (including access to Skillful training and toolkits), outplacement services (including WARN planning and worker transition services), and employee training resources (on‑the‑job training, incumbent worker training, Employment Training Panel funding, apprenticeships).
- Career seeker services: personalized advising, workshops, training navigation and financial assistance, and targeted services for veterans and spouses.
- Young adult services (ages 17–24): personalized coaching, “earn while you learn” paid internships across multiple fields, career exploration, job search tools, college transition and financial aid guidance, and additional supports.

Strategically, NOVAworks applies a sector‑strategy approach—deeply engaging employers to co‑shape training aligned with real hiring needs, investing in pathways to quality jobs (with a focus on underrepresented workers), and piloting innovative solutions such as skills‑based hiring, apprenticeships, and new training models. Its Launch Lab function highlights program design and testing to meet evolving talent demands. The NOVAworks Workforce Board represents a consortium of eight jurisdictions (the County of San Mateo and the seven Santa Clara County cities served), providing regional governance and alignment.

Distinctive aspects
- Public workforce system backbone with comprehensive, no‑cost services for both employers and job seekers.
- Sector strategy and employer co‑design of training; strong emphasis on skills‑based hiring and inclusive career pathways.
- NOVAworks Job Connector offers exclusive local profiles/resumes and real‑time candidate matching by required skills.
- Integrated outplacement services, including WARN support and co‑hosted (NOVAworks/EDD) layoff‑assistance webinars.
- “Earn while you learn” paid internships for young adults, with wages handled by NOVAworks, lowering employer barriers.
