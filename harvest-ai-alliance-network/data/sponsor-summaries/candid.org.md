## Candid — Company Profile

Candid is a U.S.–based nonprofit organization formed in 2019 from the merger of GuideStar and Foundation Center. Its mission is to get people “the information you need to do good.” Candid collects, cleans, enriches, and distributes the most comprehensive data on the U.S. social sector to help nonprofits, funders, businesses, analysts, and technical solution providers make informed decisions and connect more efficiently.

Core focus and offerings
- Candid search (research and discovery)
  - Up-to-date, enriched data on nonprofits and grants (beyond IRS Form 990s), searchable by mission, programs, location, leadership, finances, and decision-makers.
  - Designed to help nonprofits find funders and funders find nonprofits that align with their priorities; includes financial overviews, trends, and grants history.
- Seals of Transparency (nonprofit profiles)
  - Free program enabling nonprofits to claim a profile, tell their story, and earn a Candid Seal of Transparency.
  - Seal holders’ profiles appear on 200+ charitable and giving platforms; on average, Seal holders attract 62% more in contributions (per a cited study).
- Data products and integrations
  - APIs for real-time data integration (nonprofits, foundations, grants, and news) into corporate, foundation, and platform workflows.
  - Custom data services (tailored datasets) for analysis, verification, compliance, and market intelligence.
  - Data powers a network of 200+ giving platforms, corporate giving programs, donor-advised funds, and more.

Who Candid serves
- Nonprofits: Fundraising tools, funder discovery (grants data, funder recommendations), learning resources (webinars, proposal-writing training, sample documents), and Seals of Transparency to increase donor trust.
- Funders & donors: Comprehensive nonprofit profiles (mission, programs, geography, populations served, leadership, demographics, financials) to streamline grantmaking; compliance and verification data; sharing grants data to strengthen sector-wide insights.
- Business & financial services: Prospecting, onboarding, and verification of nonprofit clients; compliance status checks; grants received; impact metrics; integration via APIs and datasets.
- Technical solution providers: Embed nonprofit and grants data to power features in philanthropy, giving, and compliance solutions.

Scale and data coverage
- 1.9 million organization profiles (IRS data enhanced with Candid’s exclusive collection and enrichment).
- 3 million annual grant transactions standardized, tagged, and organized for analysis.
- $180 billion in annual grant dollars represented, available at itemized or aggregated levels.

Approach and differentiation
- Transparency-first: Candid champions data transparency to drive better decisions and outcomes across the social sector.
- Enriched, timely data: Goes beyond public IRS data by collecting directly from organizations, standardizing, and updating continuously; offers real-time access via APIs.
- Actionable insights across the ecosystem: Data is designed to power discovery (Candid search), decision-making (grants and financial insights), compliance/verification (daily monitoring of multiple federal and state sources for funders), and platform integrations at scale.

Vision
- A social sector capable of tackling the critical challenges of our time by giving every stakeholder the information they need to play their part.

Distinctive aspects
- Seals of Transparency program and broad distribution: Claimed profiles and Seal status are syndicated to 200+ giving sites, amplifying nonprofit reach and donor trust.
- Depth and breadth of U.S. nonprofit and grants data, standardized for research and decision-making (including leadership/staff demographics where available).
- Enterprise-grade integrations and compliance monitoring for funders (e.g., daily-verified data across multiple federal and state sources).
- Formed from the merger of two sector-defining entities (GuideStar and Foundation Center), consolidating nonprofit profiles with grants and philanthropy intelligence.
