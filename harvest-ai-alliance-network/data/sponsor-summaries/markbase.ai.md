Here is an authoritative profile of MarkBase based on a crawl of its public site.

Summary
MarkBase LLC builds MarkBase, an AI-powered marketing assistant designed to replace traditional agency workflows by analyzing data, generating insights, and executing marketing actions in one platform. Positioned as “your strategist, analyst, writer, and marketing exec—all in one,” MarkBase connects to major marketing and analytics platforms to provide no-fluff reporting, real-time analytics, and direct, actionable answers to business questions.

Core focus and product
- Core offering: MarkBase (AI Marketing Assistant)
  - Consolidates strategy, analysis, content generation, and execution within one system.
  - Connects to Google Analytics, Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads and other providers via official APIs to analyze performance, campaigns, audiences, and market data.
  - Supports competitor and market analysis via licensed data sources (e.g., SEMrush).
  - Includes tools for contact discovery and email outreach through verified business data partners.
  - Provides a natural-language Q&A interface to deliver accurate, actionable guidance on campaigns, performance, content, and strategy.

Target users and industries
- Businesses and marketers seeking to centralize performance analytics, insights, and execution without relying on traditional agencies.
- Organizations that want real-time, decision-ready answers and marketing automation across paid media and analytics ecosystems.

Unique value propositions
- “Built by marketers” with agency experience; designed to “replace” agency overhead with practical, action-oriented intelligence.
- Real-time, no-fluff insights with direct, actionable answers rather than generic dashboards.
- Consolidation of roles (strategist/analyst/writer/executive) into one AI assistant to reduce tool and vendor sprawl.
- Strong privacy and security stance: minimum-scope data access, no resale of data, and no retention longer than necessary.

Security, privacy, and compliance highlights
- Company: MarkBase LLC (Woodland Hills, CA, USA).
- Hosting and security:
  - Data hosted on AWS; encryption in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (AES-256).
  - Role-based access controls, regular audits, and vulnerability testing.
- Data handling:
  - Uses secure OAuth for third-party integrations with only required permissions.
  - No selling, renting, or trading of user data; data deleted when integrations disconnect or accounts are removed.
  - AI queries processed in real time; not stored or reused for model training.
  - Data retention: account deletion removes data within 30 days (backups purged within 90 days).
- Compliance frameworks cited: GDPR, CCPA, Google API Services User Data Policy, LinkedIn Platform Terms, Meta Marketing API.
