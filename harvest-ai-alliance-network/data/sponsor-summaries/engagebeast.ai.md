## Engage Beast — Company Profile

Engage Beast LLC develops Engage Beast, an AI-powered social engagement platform that centralizes customer interactions and automates responses across major social and messaging channels. Positioned as “Let AI handle the replies. You handle the growth,” the product unifies DMs, comments, and chats into a single inbox, learns a brand’s tone, and auto‑replies in real time to reduce response lag and manual effort.

Core focus and offerings
- AI-generated smart replies
  - Learns the brand voice and automatically responds to DMs and comments (real-time AI replies) across supported channels.
  - Designed to reduce manual workload while preserving on-brand communication.
- Unified multi-channel inbox
  - Consolidates messages, comments, and chats from multiple platforms into one interface.
  - Supported platforms (via official APIs): Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, YouTube, Google My Business, LinkedIn, TikTok. 
- Fast onboarding and “start free”
  - “It takes minutes to unify your channels and let AI do the rest,” with a start-free option to trial the product.

Target customers
- Businesses and teams managing high social media volume that need faster, consistent engagement (e.g., SMBs, brands, marketing teams).
- Agencies and service providers (via the Partner Program) serving clients with social engagement challenges.

Unique value propositions
- Real-time AI replies that adopt a brand’s tone and handle routine engagement at scale.
- Single, powerful inbox to manage cross-platform comments and messages.
- Minimal permissions approach and strong privacy stance: official API integrations, real-time AI processing without storing conversation data for training.

Partner Program
- Recurring revenue for referrals: partners earn 15% of all revenue from referred customers on a recurring (monthly) basis, for the lifetime of the customer.
- Engage Beast handles onboarding, AI setup, multi-channel integration, and support.
