## LinkedIn — Company Profile

LinkedIn is the world’s largest professional network, with more than 1 billion members across 200+ countries and territories. Its vision is to “create economic opportunity for every member of the global workforce,” and its mission is to “connect the world’s professionals to make them more productive and successful.” Founded in 2002 and launched in 2003, the company operates a diversified business spanning membership subscriptions, advertising sales, and recruitment solutions. LinkedIn has been part of Microsoft since its acquisition in December 2016 and is led by CEO Ryan Roslansky.

Core focus and offerings
- Professional network and community
  - A global platform for members to build professional identities, connect, and engage with opportunities, content, and communities.
- Business solutions and revenue lines (as described by LinkedIn)
  - Membership subscriptions
  - Advertising solutions (for marketers to reach professional audiences)
  - Recruitment solutions (to help organizations find and hire talent)
- Learning and skills development
  - LinkedIn Learning supports workforce upskilling and corporate learning programs highlighted in customer stories (e.g., LEGO).
- Customer outcomes and case studies (Business LinkedIn)
  - Organizations report improved hiring scalability, higher-quality candidates, accelerated prospecting, and significantly improved ad performance and sales productivity.
- News and product updates (News LinkedIn)
  - Ongoing announcements on business highlights and product innovation, including AI initiatives like the LinkedIn Generative AI application tech stack and “Hiring Assistant,” LinkedIn’s first AI agent for recruiters (globally available in English).

Strategy and differentiation
- Scale and data advantage
  - Unmatched professional graph (1B+ members) powering hiring, marketing, sales, and learning solutions.
- Integrated, AI-powered capabilities
  - A generative AI application stack and AI agents (e.g., Hiring Assistant) designed to improve recruiter efficiency and outcomes.
- Part of Microsoft’s ecosystem
  - Since 2016, LinkedIn has operated under Microsoft, pairing the “professional cloud” with the “professional network” for broader enterprise impact.

Leadership and heritage
- Founded by Reid Hoffman and co-founders; launched in 2003.
- Microsoft acquisition completed in 2016.
- CEO: Ryan Roslansky.

Culture and careers
- Careers LinkedIn highlights benefits across wellness, family support, community engagement (InDay, volunteering, donation matching), and professional development (speaker series, education reimbursement).
- Employee testimonials emphasize transformative career paths, inclusive culture, and impact-driven work aligned with LinkedIn’s vision.

Distinctive aspects
- Global scale: 1B+ members and presence in 200+ countries and territories.
- Diversified revenue: memberships, ads, and recruitment solutions.
- AI-forward recruiting: Hiring Assistant—an AI agent for recruiters—plus an internal generative AI stack supporting product innovation.
- Microsoft integration: Combines the professional network with Microsoft’s professional cloud.
