## Adobe — Company Profile

Adobe is a global software company focused on “changing the world through personalized digital experiences.” Since 1982, Adobe has built category‑defining creative and document technologies (e.g., PostScript, Photoshop, PDF) and today empowers creators, students, small businesses, global enterprises, and nonprofits to ideate, collaborate, and deliver exceptional digital content—now enhanced with integrated, responsible AI.

Core focus and offerings
- Creative and content platforms
  - Creative Cloud: 20+ apps including Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, Adobe Express, and more, bundled with Adobe Firefly creative AI features for images, video, and audio.
  - Acrobat Pro: Document creation, editing, collaboration, and PDF workflows.
- Generative AI
  - Adobe Firefly: AI built to “amplify human ingenuity,” integrated across Adobe tools to accelerate creativity and productivity while enabling personalization at scale.
  - Commercial safety focus: Firefly models are trained on data designed to enable commercially safe outputs; Adobe advances transparency via the Content Authenticity Initiative to establish industry standards for content provenance.
  - Multi‑model support: Site messaging indicates users can “Generate with AI models from Adobe, Google, OpenAI, ElevenLabs, and more,” highlighting access to select partner models alongside Adobe’s.
- Outcomes Adobe emphasizes
  - Unleashing creativity
  - Accelerating productivity
  - Powering digital business
  - Creating positive change for communities and customers worldwide

Target users and industries
- Creatives and students seeking professional-grade tools for content creation.
- Small businesses and global enterprises requiring design, document, and marketing content workflows.
- Nonprofits and public sector organizations aiming to scale communications and digital experiences.

Mission and approach to AI
- Mission: “Changing the world through personalized digital experiences.”
- AI philosophy: Designed to unlock users’ potential, developed responsibly and transparently, and integrated seamlessly into existing workflows to fuel creativity and productivity while enabling enterprise‑grade personalization.

Heritage and milestones
- Making history since 1982: Innovations include the debut of PostScript, the introduction of Photoshop, the invention of PDF, and establishing the digital marketing software category—followed by continued advancements in generative AI and workflow integration.

Distinctive aspects
- Seamless, native AI across flagship apps (e.g., Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, Express, Acrobat).
- Firefly’s “commercially safe” design and leadership in content authenticity and provenance (Content Authenticity Initiative).
- Indication of multi‑model access (Adobe + partners like Google, OpenAI, ElevenLabs), broadening creative and productivity possibilities from within Adobe experiences.
- A combination of creative, document, and (broader) digital experience capabilities, positioned to personalize at scale across audiences and industries.
