## Seoul AI Hub (서울 AI 허브)

Seoul AI Hub is the Seoul Metropolitan City’s core urban R&D campus hub dedicated to artificial intelligence. It supports AI-focused talent development and startup/company growth through programs, facilities, and community-building. The site positions the Hub as a central node in the Yangjae Innovation District, with offerings that span incubation/residency, education, infrastructure support, and ecosystem news.

Core focus
- AI talent development and company incubation: Specialized support for AI entrepreneurs, early-stage ventures, and growth-stage companies.
- Program delivery: Recruitment and execution of AI commercialization programs, workshops, and seminars.
- Ecosystem platform: Serves as a central point for AI-related news, partner activities, and government/related agency announcements.

Key programs and services (examples)
- Growth support programs:
  - High-performance computing (HPC) infrastructure support for AI companies (2025 program; recruitment and activity windows posted on the site).
  - AI Transformation (AX) support program (2025 program; consortium participation).
- Facility access:
  - Office options (open co-working and independent offices) with published rental and maintenance rates.
  - Meeting rooms, lecture rooms, lounge; facility reservation and usage guidance.
- Resident companies:
  - Public directory and news for affiliated/resident companies.
- Information and communications:
  - Hub announcements, press releases, government/related agency updates, and company brochures.

Incubation/residency overview (입주 안내)
- Eligibility:
  - (Pre-)founders planning AI-related businesses; early-stage AI startups/SMEs/research labs with AI-focused technology.
- Selection process:
  - Document review → Presentation (e.g., 10-minute pitch + 10-minute Q&A) → Results posted on website.
- Recruitment:
  - Non-regular (based on graduations/space expansion); announced on the Hub website.
- Office types and costs:
  - Open co-working seats: monthly rent 60,000 KRW per seat; maintenance 9,200 KRW per seat.
  - Independent offices: monthly rent 3,900 KRW per m²; maintenance 1,840 KRW per m².
  - Notes: 6 months’ fees paid in advance; VAT separate.
- Benefits:
  - Access to Hub facilities (meeting rooms, lecture rooms, lounge), networking/collaboration, and growth programs.

Target users and regions
- Audience: AI-focused (pre-)founders, startups, SMEs, and prospective AI talent.
- Region served: Seoul (Yangjae Innovation District); operates as a city-level AI hub.

Communications and languages

Especially distinctive aspects
- Municipal AI hub anchoring Seoul’s urban R&D campus, with a strong emphasis on both talent development and company growth.
- Structured, transparent residency framework with defined eligibility, selection steps, and published cost schedules.
- Program portfolio that includes practical infrastructure support (e.g., HPC) and AI transformation initiatives.
