## IBM — Company Profile

IBM is a global technology and consulting company focused on helping enterprises “lead in the AI era” through hybrid cloud, AI, software, infrastructure, and advisory services. Its mission is to be a catalyst that makes the world work better, delivering positive impact globally through business ethics, environmental commitment, and responsible technology.

Core focus and offerings
- AI and data for business
  - AI agents and assistants to automate complex workflows.
  - AI models tailored for enterprise scale and cost-efficiency.
  - Data for AI: access, integration, governance, and security of enterprise data, wherever it resides.
  - Analytics to support data-driven decisions.
- Hybrid cloud and automation
  - Hybrid cloud management to run workloads where and when needed.
  - Automation solutions to increase productivity and manage costs.
  - Infrastructure modernization across applications, servers, and storage to integrate with hybrid cloud and AI.
- Software and security
  - Enterprise software for modernization, prediction, automation, and security on AI and hybrid cloud.
  - Cybersecurity and identity-centric solutions to secure hybrid cloud and AI environments.
- Consulting and partnerships
  - IBM Consulting® to design, build, and operate high-performing businesses.
  - Strategic Partnerships for co-development, co-marketing, and co-selling of end-to-end solutions with leading technology providers.
  - Red Hat to increase productivity, reduce costs, and improve business outcomes across hybrid cloud.

Mission, scale, and reach
- Mission: Be a catalyst that makes the world work better (positive impact through ethics, environmental commitment, and responsible tech).
- Scale metrics (fast facts):
  - 300,000+ employees across 170+ countries.
  - 2,000+ businesses in the IBM Partner Plus program.
  - 660+ products across solution areas.
  - 19 research facilities across 12 labs on 6 continents (demonstrating long-standing R&D depth).
- Origin: Over a century at the forefront of computing and enterprise technology, blending intelligence, innovation, and science to improve business and society.

Notable partnerships and examples
- IBM + Anthropic: Partnership integrating Claude into IBM products (expands generative AI options for clients).
- Scuderia Ferrari HP and IBM: Built a new digital destination for F1 fans, achieving 2× daily active users and a 35% increase in time spent in-app (example of IBM’s applied digital/AI expertise).
- Strategic Partnerships: Collaborations with ecosystem partners to deliver innovative, industry-spanning solutions.

Leadership
- Chairman, President and CEO: Arvind Krishna (and a broader executive team across Software, Infrastructure, Consulting, Research, Strategy, Legal, HR, and Ecosystem).

Distinctive aspects
- Enterprise AI at scale with strong governance and security posture (data- and identity-centric approaches).
- Hybrid cloud leadership powered by Red Hat and a broad software portfolio.
- Global research footprint (19 facilities) and deep industry expertise via IBM Consulting.
- Extensive partner ecosystem (Partner Plus program; co-innovation with leading AI partners like Anthropic).
