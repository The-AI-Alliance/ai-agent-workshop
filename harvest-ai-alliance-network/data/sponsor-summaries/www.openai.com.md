## OpenAI — Company Profile

OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company focused on developing frontier AI systems and making them useful and accessible through consumer products (e.g., ChatGPT) and developer platforms (APIs, tools). Its public materials highlight continuous model advancement, responsible AI development, and global expansion via offices and partnerships.

- Flagship products and services (selected from site index/sitemaps)

Target users and audiences
- Consumers and knowledge workers using ChatGPT for search, assistance, and creation.
