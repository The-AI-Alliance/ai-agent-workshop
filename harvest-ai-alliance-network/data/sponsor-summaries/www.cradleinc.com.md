## Hyundai CRADLE (Center for Robotic-Augmented Design in Living Experiences)

Hyundai CRADLE is the global open innovation and corporate venture arm of Hyundai Motor Group. According to its official site, CRADLE connects Hyundai Motor Group with groundbreaking ideas worldwide to advance mobility and improve quality of life. It combines venture capital investment, proof-of-concept development, startup scouting, and ecosystem integration to accelerate transformative mobility solutions.

Core focus and activities
- Venture capital investments: Backs pioneers developing next-generation technologies.
- Development of new ideas: Builds proofs of concept (PoCs) to validate and enhance the viability of innovations.
- Scouting and intelligence: Identifies exceptional talent and insights shaping the future.
- Ecosystem integration: Bridges startups, partners, and Hyundai Motor Group to scale solutions in mobility.

Positioning and value proposition
- CRADLE serves as a conduit between Hyundai Motor Group and the global startup ecosystem, enabling rapid experimentation (via PoCs) and strategic investment to bring innovations into real-world mobility.
- Its integrated model—investment, validation, scouting, and integration—aims to shorten time-to-value for promising technologies and embed them within Hyundai’s broader mobility initiatives.

Distinctive aspects
- Integrated innovation model: Combines corporate VC, PoC development, and ecosystem integration—beyond capital alone.
- Global footprint: Six innovation hubs spanning North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia-Pacific.
- Clear mobility mandate: Explicit focus on accelerating transformative mobility solutions, aligned with Hyundai Motor Group’s mission.
