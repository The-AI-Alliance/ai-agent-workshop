## Google — Company Profile

Google is a global technology company whose mission is “to organize the world’s information and make it universally accessible and useful.” Founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin at Stanford (1995–1998), Google grew from a link-based search engine into a company delivering hundreds of products to billions of people worldwide. Its headquarters (the Googleplex) is in Mountain View, California, with offices around the globe.

Core business focus and offerings
- Search and AI assistants
  - Google Search and the Google app; Gemini, a personal AI assistant with features like Gemini Live, Canvas, image and video generation.
- Maps, Android, and Pixel ecosystem
  - Google Maps (now enhanced with Gemini for richer navigation and place insights).
  - Android platform and devices (Pixel phones, watches, earbuds, tablets) with AI features across the portfolio.
- Chrome and ChromeOS
  - Chrome browser, Chromebooks/ChromeOS for consumer and education use cases.
- Productivity, creativity, and experiments
  - Google Workspace referenced on About; NotebookLM (personalized AI research assistant).
  - Labs experiments including Flow (AI filmmaking tool) and broader Google Labs initiatives.
- Additional consumer and developer offerings
  - Products span communications (Gmail, Contacts), finance (Google Finance), travel (Flights), health and wearables (Fitbit), device security and discovery (Find Hub), digital wellbeing, and more.
  - Product guidance and usage permissions via the Google Brand Resource Center.

Commitments and values
- Protecting users: Industry-leading security, responsible data practices, and user controls.
- Building and deploying AI responsibly: Guided by Google’s AI Principles, safety practices, and policy engagement with experts and governments.
- Expanding opportunity: Programs such as Grow with Google, Google.org, and learning resources to broaden access to skills, knowledge, and support for nonprofits.
- Building for everyone: Focus on accessibility, belonging, and making AI beneficial for all communities.

Recent news and milestones
- Quantum research: “Quantum Echoes” algorithm, a step toward real-world quantum applications.
- Gemini in Maps: More conversational, context-aware navigation and place information.
- Pixel portfolio: Latest device lineup (including Pixel 10) expands hardware innovations with integrated AI features.

Brand use and resources
- The Brand Resource Center provides guidelines for using Google logos, product icons, and brand elements, including when permission is required.

Headquarters and global presence
- HQ: Mountain View, California (Googleplex).
- Offices: Worldwide; site materials showcase locations and global impact.
