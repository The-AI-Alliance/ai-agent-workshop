## Hootsuite — Company Profile

Hootsuite is a social media management and performance platform that pioneered the category in 2008 and “has been leading the global social space ever since.” It helps organizations turn social insights into clear actions and measurable business impact—spanning social marketing, listening, engagement, analytics, and customer service—all from one interface. Hootsuite serves “hundreds of thousands of customers,” from small businesses to the world’s largest organizations.

Core focus and products
- Creation and publishing
  - AI-assisted content generation (OwlyGPT, OwlyWriter) for strategy, captions, scripts, and messages; AI hashtag suggestions; repurposing top posts; holiday content automation.
  - Visual tools: Built-in Canva templates; stock photos and GIPHY library; media editing and sizing; whiteboard planning space; asset/content library.
  - Scheduling and workflow: Bulk Composer (up to 350 posts), content calendar (list/calendar view), post approvals and internal comments (multi-user), recommended times to publish, link shortening (Ow.ly/Bitly), and HootBio (link-in-bio).
  - Network coverage: Publish/schedule across major social networks from one place.
- Engagement and customer service
  - Unified inbox for public and private messaging across networks; DM automations (e.g., IG keyword DMs); saved/suggested replies; message routing and assignments; tags/topics and related automations; team settings and internal collaboration.
- Analytics and reporting
  - Hundreds of metrics; customizable reports and templates; post performance; cross-network reports; best times to publish charts.
  - Benchmarking: Industry benchmarking and competitor tracking (watchlists); social performance scores with personalized recommendations.
  - Reporting workflows: Exporting (PDF, PPT, CSV/XLSX), report scheduling, share within Hootsuite or via external email; ROI reporting for paid and organic (Enterprise).
- Social listening
  - Search and track brand mentions, trending topics, and competitors; use built-in AI tools to convert insights into content quickly.
- Integrations ecosystem
  - Consolidates marketing stack “into a single tab,” integrating CRM, project management, collaboration, content, and more.
  - Named integrations include: Salesforce, Mailchimp, HubSpot, Marketo, Slack, Microsoft Teams, Monday.com, Wrike, Asana, Airtable, Basecamp, Dropbox, Zapier, Google My Business, Google Translate, Dribbble, Figma, Lately AI, Proofpoint, Adobe, Widen, Upfluence, Vidyard, and others.

Plans and target customers
- Standard, Advanced, and Enterprise tiers (per-user annual billing or enterprise contract), scaling from single-user to multi-team, multi-network collaboration.
- Designed for social teams and cross‑functional stakeholders (marketing, CX, comms), from SMBs to enterprise—supported by features for governance, workflows, analytics, and integrations.

Positioning and value proposition
- “Social media performance engine”: Unlocks the value of social relationships by translating social signals into action and measurable results.
- Breadth + depth: Creation, scheduling, engagement, listening, analytics, and ROI measurement in one platform—with a large library of integrations.
- AI-infused workflows: Strategy advice, content generation, caption enhancement, hashtag suggestions, content repurposing, trend-informed prompts, and inbox automations.

Notable details (from Hootsuite’s site)

- Customer scope: “Hundreds of thousands of customers,” from small businesses to the largest organizations.
- Unique AI tools and branding: OwlyGPT and OwlyWriter referenced throughout features and pricing.
