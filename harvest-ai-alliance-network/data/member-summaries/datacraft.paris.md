## datacraft — Organization Summary

datacraft is a Paris-based learning and coworking club for data scientists and data engineers. It accelerates the implementation of data and AI projects by enabling peer-to-peer training, sharing of best practices, and cross-fertilization among practitioners. The first Base opened in February 2020 in Paris, and the organization hosts a residency of 600+ high-level data science researchers and freelancers. Its mission is to contribute to an efficient and responsible use of data.

Core focus and activities
- Membership club for data teams: Weekly technical workshops with peers; access to shared tools (Slack, GitHub) and to physical premises (“Base”) in Paris.
- Community and talent hub: Member companies connect with researchers and freelancers in residence for mission work and recruitment; job board available.
- Upskilling and acculturation:
  - Training programs on AI and generative AI for generalists and engineers/scientists, delivered in partnership with Sorbonne University (Sorbonne Center for Artificial Intelligence, Paris); includes e-learning capsules and discovery pathways aligned with France 2030.
  - Specialized activities: data governance workshops, monthly evenings for CDOs & HR, a digital escape game, technical meetups and hackathons (including “Hackathon as a service”), and thematic streams (Frugal AI, Trustable AI, Generative AI).
- Project acceleration and collaboration: Streams and workshops aimed at improving deliverable quality and speeding up data-driven initiatives in member organizations.

Target users and industries
- Corporate data teams (data scientists, data engineers) and their organizations.
- CDOs and HR leaders seeking community, governance practices, and talent.
- Researchers and freelance experts looking for missions with member companies.

Value proposition
- Practitioner-first, peer-learning environment that shortens the path from concept to production for data/AI projects.
- Access to a curated community of experts, a large pool of resident researchers/freelancers, and member events/programming designed to foster responsible, high-impact data science.
- Formal training and acculturation offering through a recognized academic partner (Sorbonne University).
