## Core42 — Company Overview

Core42 is a G42 company focused on sovereign AI and cloud solutions that help individuals, enterprises, and nations unlock the full potential of AI. Its mission is to accelerate customers’ AI transformation through sovereign cloud, AI infrastructure, and Cloud & AI Managed Services.

Business focus and offerings
- Sovereign AI and Cloud
  - Sovereign Public Cloud: public-cloud solution tailored for the UAE public sector and regulated industries; combines Microsoft Azure hyperscale capabilities with UAE-specific controls via Core42’s Insight application to meet data sovereignty, security, and compliance needs.
  - Signature Private Cloud: hyperscaler-grade capabilities delivered from fully owned and operated data centers in the UAE, emphasizing security, controlled connectivity, and data/operations/technology sovereignty.
- Core42 AI Cloud (HPC for AI/ML): high-performance, silicon-diverse infrastructure optimized for AI workloads.
- Compass platform
  - Compass API: unified access to leading foundation models (including OpenAI’s open-weight GPT‑OSS models) across multiple silicon platforms with sovereign deployment options.
  - Compass Management Portal: single pane to access models, monitor performance, and manage the Compass API.
  - Compass Chat: AI assistant leveraging GPT‑4o and Jais 30B with robust security and bilingual support.
- Cloud & AI Managed Services: delivery and operations expertise with agile service delivery, customer-centric engagement, and scalable, flexible support.

Target customers and industries
- Governments and regulated industries (e.g., public sector, healthcare, finance, national security) seeking in-country, sovereign-enabled AI and cloud deployments.
- Enterprises, researchers, and developers needing secure, high-performance AI infrastructure and managed access to state-of-the-art models.

Value proposition
- Sovereign-by-design: in-country controls for data, operations, and technology to satisfy regulatory and national requirements.
- Silicon-diverse performance: ability to align workloads to optimal compute for price-performance and scalability.
- Unified model access: Compass API and portal simplify adoption, monitoring, and governance of top-tier AI models.

Notable announcements and metrics
- GPT‑OSS on Core42 AI Cloud: OpenAI’s open-weight GPT‑OSS models (e.g., 20B and 120B) available globally via the Compass API; designed to deliver up to 3,000 tokens/second per user for low-latency, enterprise-scale inference, with sovereign-ready deployment options (August 13, 2025; Abu Dhabi, UAE).
- Whitepapers/resources emphasize data sovereignty for UAE public sector and regulated industries, migration best practices, and the competitive advantages of Core42’s Sovereign Public Cloud.
