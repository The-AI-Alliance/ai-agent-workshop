## Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) — Organization Overview

MBZUAI is a graduate research university in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates, dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence as a global force for humanity. Established by the UAE’s leadership and officially licensed on March 10, 2020, by the UAE Ministry of Education’s Commission for Academic Accreditation (CAA), the university focuses on world‑class research and education in computer vision, machine learning, natural language processing, computational biology, and robotics.

Core focus and activities
- Research and education: MBZUAI conducts fundamental and applied AI research and offers degree programs (graduate, with undergraduate programs in development) designed to educate innovators and leaders in AI for the UAE and the world.
- Innovation ecosystem: The university fosters entrepreneurship through startup support, technology transfer, industry partnerships, executive and professional training, and collaborations with public and private sectors.
- Strategic think tank: MBZUAI supports the UAE’s National Strategy for Artificial Intelligence 2031, helping position Abu Dhabi as a global hub for the AI community and contributing to a knowledge-driven economy.

Programs and academic development
- Degree programs: Offers graduate degree programs with full scholarships for exceptional students worldwide; plans to introduce first‑of‑its‑kind undergraduate AI programs.
- Executive education: Launched the MBZUAI Executive Program to broaden executive and professional AI training.
- Campus: Located in Masdar City, Abu Dhabi, with virtual campus tours available.

Governance and leadership
- Board of Trustees: Newly constituted and chaired by His Excellency Khaldoon Al Mubarak; includes distinguished leaders from industry, government, and academia.
- University leadership: President and University Professor Eric Xing; Provost and Professor of Natural Language Processing Timothy Baldwin.

Mission, vision, and strategic objectives
- Mission: Establish and continually evolve interdisciplinary, collaborative R&D in AI while educating students to be innovators and leaders who advance technology and enterprise in the UAE and globally.
- Vision: Drive excellence in the creation, transfer, and use of AI knowledge to foster economic growth and position Abu Dhabi as a global AI hub.
- Objectives: Attract and develop top AI talent; lead Abu Dhabi’s AI‑based knowledge economy; build real business applications with industry/public sector; and be the birthplace of high‑tech innovation and AI startups in the UAE and MENA region.

Partnerships and collaborations
- Partnership model: Academic partners (local/international institutions, virtual platforms, think tanks), industry partners (joint research, technology development, labs), and operations partners (service providers, agencies).
- Collaboration areas: Joint/sponsored research, consultancy, internships and recruitment, curriculum co-development, and AI events and conferences.

Growth and expansion plans
- Academic scope: Planned growth beyond core AI disciplines to public health, life sciences, decision science, energy and environment, and advanced computing — with AI as the foundation.
- Education pipeline: Expansion of executive training and exploration of integrating AI into K‑12 curricula in alignment with national priorities.

Distinctive aspects
- Nationally strategic role supporting UAE AI priorities and Abu Dhabi’s positioning as a global AI hub.
- Full scholarships for qualified students, attracting top global talent.
- Rapid institutional progress (since establishment in 2019/2020) with global recognition in key AI subfields.
- Structured partnerships bridging research, development, and operations to accelerate innovation.

Headquarters and region
- Headquarters: Abu Dhabi, UAE (Masdar City campus).
- Regions served: UAE and international academic, research, and industry communities.
