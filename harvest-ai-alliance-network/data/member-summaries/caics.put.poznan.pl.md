## Interdisciplinary Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity (CAICS) — Overview

CAICS is a research and innovation center within Poznan University of Technology (Politechnika Poznańska) focused on artificial intelligence (AI) and cybersecurity. Its mission is to conduct research, run R&D projects, and deliver competencies in AI and cybersecurity by collaborating with academia, industry, and public-sector stakeholders.

Focus and offerings
- Research and R&D delivery in AI and cybersecurity, with strong industry and public-sector collaboration.
- Expert services and consulting for economic, scientific, and administrative organizations.
- Grant development and project management support (national and international consortia).
- Education and workforce development: training for industry, student education, implementation doctorates, technical workshops, and conferences.

Core competencies (selected)
- Security: IoT, networks, big data; secure communications between unmanned vehicles; anomaly detection in network traffic.
- Smart cities and critical infrastructure: risk estimation; multimodal data integration/analysis; data quality; data lakes/warehouses/big data.
- Applied AI/ML:
  - Healthcare: medical image recognition; disease risk estimation.
  - Energy: predictive models for energy consumption.
  - NLP: text/sentiment analysis; fake-news detection.
  - Engineering: autonomous vehicles; transport robotics; robotics/IoT in sustainable agriculture; real-time vision.
  - Affective computing: automatic emotion recognition.
